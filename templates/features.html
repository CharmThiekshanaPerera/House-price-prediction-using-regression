Features

Our prediction model uses the following features to estimate house prices:

  • Number of Bedrooms: The total number of bedrooms in the house.
  • Grade: The overall grade given to the house based on its construction and design.
  • Has Basement: Indicates whether the house has a basement (1 for Yes, 0 for No).
  • Living Area (in mÂ²): The total living area of the house in square meters.
  • Renovated: Indicates whether the house has been renovated (1 for Yes, 0 for No).
  • Nice View: Indicates whether the house has a nice view (1 for Yes, 0 for No).
  • Perfect Condition: Indicates whether the house is in perfect condition (1 for Yes, 0 for No).
  • Number of Bathrooms: The total number of bathrooms in the house.
  • Has Lavatory: Indicates whether the house has a lavatory (1 for Yes, 0 for No).
  • Single Floor: Indicates whether the house is a single-floor building (1 for Yes, 0 for No).
  • Month: The month when the house was listed.
  • Quartile Zone: The quartile zone of the house location.
  • Year: The year when the house was built.
